What is weak or missing
1. Title is decent, but slightly generic

Audit your AI search visibility and rank higher in AI search | AnswerMonk

Problem:

repeats “AI search”
sounds a bit SEO-generic
less crisp than it could be

Better options:

AI Search Visibility Audit for Brands and Agencies | AnswerMonk
Track and Improve Your Brand’s Visibility in ChatGPT and AI Search | AnswerMonk
AI Visibility Audits for ChatGPT, Gemini, and AI Search | AnswerMonk
2. Meta description is underspecified

“One-sentence summary” is too vague. It should explicitly include:

what you measure
where
who it is for
outcome

Example:
Measure how your brand appears in ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and AI search, benchmark competitors, and find actions to improve citations and visibility.

3. JSON-LD choice is not enough by itself

Organization + SoftwareApplication is good, but I’d also consider:

WebSite
WebPage
BreadcrumbList on internal pages
FAQPage where relevant

Not mandatory, but stronger.

4. Missing proof pages

This is the biggest gap.

You still need at least:

/sample-report
/case-studies or at least 1–2 case pages
/examples or /audit-example
maybe /templates/ai-visibility-report

Without proof, the site remains mostly explanatory, not persuasive.

5. Missing commercial intent pages

You have one feature page, but not enough direct demand capture.

I would add:

/chatgpt-visibility-audit
/llm-seo-audit
/ai-visibility-tool
/brand-mention-tracking-for-ai-search or similar if that is core

Not all at once, but at least 2–3 of these.

6. Missing “for Dubai / GCC / MENA” if that is your wedge

If regional relevance matters, you should not hide it.

Potential pages:

/ai-search-visibility-for-dubai-agencies
/geo-audit-for-gcc-brands

Only if this is actually your go-to-market wedge.

7. “Want me to go ahead?” is too optimistic unless content quality is real

These 20 changes help only if the pages are:

unique
useful
non-thin
internally linked
written with actual depth

If they are shallow SEO filler, the gain will be limited.

Bottom line

This is a solid Phase 1, not a full solution.

It will likely improve:

crawlability
entity clarity
indexable footprint
category relevance

But it will not be enough on its own to make AnswerMonk look authoritative unless you add:

proof
examples
internal linking
real depth on each page

My direct verdict: go ahead, but add proof pages to the plan before shipping.
