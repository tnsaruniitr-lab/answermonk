What AI Says About NEXA · When it does recommend you

Being mentioned is one thing. What AI says about you when it does mention you determines whether a prospect clicks through or skips. Here's the language being used — and what's missing from it.

ChatGPT Prompt: Best marketing agencies in Dubai
"NEXA — a full-service digital marketing agency based in Dubai, offering SEO, PPC, social media and HubSpot services."
Problem: Functionally accurate, but generic. No differentiator, no proof point, no reason to choose NEXA over any other full-service agency. Compare to how ChatGPT describes SEO Sherpa: "Repeated MENA Search Awards recognition including Best Large SEO Agency." One has evidence. One doesn't.
Gemini Prompt: SEO agencies for ecommerce in Dubai
"NEXA is a Dubai-based digital agency with experience across multiple verticals including ecommerce."
Problem: "Experience across multiple verticals" is the weakest possible signal to an ecommerce brand. It reads as a generalist hedge. Digital Gravity gets described as "the go-to for technical SEO with deep ecommerce expertise" — specific, ownable, decisive. NEXA's description triggers no selection.
Claude Prompt: Top SEO agencies in Dubai for enterprise
"NEXA — established Dubai agency with strong inbound and HubSpot expertise, suitable for enterprise-level integrated campaigns."
Strongest engine for NEXA. Claude assigns a clearer identity — HubSpot, inbound, enterprise. This is the signal that's working. The full report shows how to replicate this language pattern on ChatGPT and Gemini.
