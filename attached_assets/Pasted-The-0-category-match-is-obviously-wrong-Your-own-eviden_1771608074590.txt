Yet the system says:

That can’t be true unless your matcher is looking for the wrong tokens (e.g., expense_management_software literal string) instead of real phrases like “expense management”, “spend management”, etc.

Root cause

Your “category match” appears to be using the internal segment key (expense_management_software, corporate_cards_provider) as the keyword to match, rather than an intent dictionary of natural synonyms.

So it’s failing even when the page clearly matches.

✅ Fix this first. Everything else is downstream noise until this is corrected.

A domain counts as “supporting” for a segment if it has ≥1 snippet with category match ≥1.

Otherwise it’s just “mention domain”.
