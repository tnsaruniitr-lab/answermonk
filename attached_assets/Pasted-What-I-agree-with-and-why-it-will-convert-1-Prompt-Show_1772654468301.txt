What I agree with (and why it will convert)

1) Prompt Showdown (2–3 prompts) — highest impact

This is the best “proof of work” because it’s checkable by the reader in 30 seconds.

It also demonstrates you’re not hand-waving with “visibility scores”; you’re mapping real buyer language to outcomes.

2) Move “How AI perceives your brand” up

This is the moment that creates urgency even when they’re doing “fine.”

Keep it tight: competitor sentence + your sentence + “what’s missing” in one line.

3) Show the real scale: 447 crawled citation pages

Strong credibility marker. But phrase it carefully:

“We crawled 447 cited pages referenced by AI answers” (so it’s clear these came from model citations, not arbitrary crawling).

4) Engine × segment heatmap

CEOs love this because it turns the whole problem into a single picture.

It naturally sets up the paid ask: “Why are we red here? What fixes it?”

Where I’d slightly disagree / tweak

A) Don’t fully remove “Top Players are doing right” — compress it into “Evidence-backed drivers”
Generic insights feel like filler, yes. But you still want 1–2 “drivers” to bridge from measurement to cause.

Keep one driver: authority source coverage (with named domains + your coverage vs competitor).

Kill anything that isn’t tied to numbers or named sources.

B) Be careful with “winner” language in Prompt Showdown
If you say “Winner: NEXA (#1)” and the reader types it and gets a different result due to model variance/localization, you risk trust loss.
Safer:

“In our runs (March 2026), top results were: …”

Or “Most frequent #1 across 5 repeats: …” if you have repeats.

C) “10 candidates per run” can confuse
It’s internally meaningful, but externally it can raise questions (“why 10?” “how chosen?”).
Better to report:

“We captured top 10 recommendations per prompt” (simple).

The best restructure (your order is basically correct)

I’d do this sequence:

Scorecard (overall + rank + top-3 + engine split)

Heatmap: Engines × Segments (3×7)

How AI describes you vs competitor (defining sentences)

Prompt Showdown (2–3 real prompts + ranks across engines)

Citation scale proof (447 cited pages crawled + 1,053 citations)

Authority sources (top 7 domains) + your coverage vs competitor

CTA: Full audit includes prompts+transcripts, per-segment breakdown, competitor narrative map, authority roadmap, prioritized fixes, retest plan.

One extra thing that will make this land harder

Add a tiny “Repro steps” box under Prompt Showdown:

“Try it yourself: paste this exact prompt into ChatGPT/Gemini/Claude.”

If you can, include the exact prompt wording + date.
